About Us

Mind Hurricane is an IIT-incubated robotics and AI startup developing next-generation autonomous systems for defence, policing, and agri-tech sectors. We combine advanced machine intelligence, embedded precision, and rugged design to build hardware that thinks — and acts — autonomously.

Born in IIT Labs

Engineers and researchers with IIT expertise in robotics, embedded systems, and AI.

AI-Powered Autonomy
